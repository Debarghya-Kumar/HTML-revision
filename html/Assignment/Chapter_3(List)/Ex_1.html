Cake (Baking)

Steps to bake a cake:

  1. Step-1:Gather your ingredients
  2. Step-2:Prepare the cake pans
  3. Step-3:Measure and Shift dry ingredients
  4. Step-4:Combine wet ingredients
  5. Step-5:Gradually add dry ingredients
  6. Step-6:Pore batter into cake pans
  7. Step-7:Bake in Preheated oven
  8. Step-8:Cool and remove from pan
  9. Step-9:Forst and decorate
  10. Step-10:Enjoy the cake.
